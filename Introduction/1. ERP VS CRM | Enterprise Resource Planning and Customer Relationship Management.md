# ERP vs CRM

## Introduction

ERP and CRM are two important business software systems used by organizations.

- ERP focuses on managing internal business processes and resources.
- CRM focuses on managing customer relationships, sales, and services.

Both systems help companies improve efficiency and productivity.

---

# What is ERP?

## ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning)

ERP is mainly related to the Enterprise Unit of a business.

It helps organizations manage:
- Resources
- Inventory
- Manufacturing
- Shipping
- Payments
- Product Planning

ERP integrates different departments into a single system.

---

## Examples of ERP Software

- SAP
- Oracle E-Business Suite
- JD Edwards
- SAGE
- Momentum

---

# What is CRM?

## CRM (Customer Relationship Management)

CRM is mainly related to:
- Sales Unit
- Service Unit

CRM helps companies:
- Maintain customer relationships
- Improve customer service
- Track opportunities
- Manage marketing and emails

---

## Examples of CRM Software

- Salesforce CRM
- Siebel CRM
- Peoplesoft CRM
- Sugar CRM
- Microsoft Dynamics CRM

---

# Difference Between ERP and CRM

| ERP | CRM |
|-----|-----|
| Related to Enterprise Unit | Related to Sales & Service Unit |
| Manages business resources | Manages customer relationships |
| Focuses on internal operations | Focuses on customers |
| Used for planning and operations | Used for sales and support |

---

# Business Units in ERP and CRM

## ERP Business Units

ERP handles enterprise operations such as:

- Inventory Management
- Resource Planning
- Product Planning
- Product Development
- Manufacturing
- Product Cost
- Shipping
- Payment

---

## CRM Sales Unit

CRM Sales Unit manages:

- Customer Information
- Marketing
- Opportunities
- Emails
- Reports

---

## CRM Service Unit

CRM Service Unit handles:

- Social Customer Service (WhatsApp, Facebook, etc.)
- Customer Center
- Emails

---

# Advantages of ERP

- Better resource management
- Improved productivity
- Centralized data
- Better planning
- Reduced operational cost

---

# Advantages of CRM

- Better customer relationship
- Improved customer service
- Increased sales opportunities
- Better communication
- Customer satisfaction

---

# Conclusion

ERP and CRM both play important roles in businesses.

- ERP improves internal business operations.
- CRM improves customer interaction and sales management.

Many companies use both ERP and CRM together for better business growth.
